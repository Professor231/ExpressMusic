OBJECTIVE:

Digital music piracy, very low revenue, and a dysfunctional music industry are some of the problems listeners and content creators face in Liberia. The objective of this company is to put an end to digital music piracy, boost revenue, and revitalize the structure of the Liberia music industry by building an online music streaming app or downloadable streaming platform (DSP).

MISSION STATEMENT:

To put an end to digital piracy and boost the revenue of the music industry, giving artists financial stability and enjoyable experiences to music consumers.

VISION STATEMENT:

To build a cost-effective, efficient, and profitable system that will encourage investment and ascertain the financial support of end-users of music. In doing so, we hold the following core values in high esteem—employee development, professionalism, transparency, communication, and customer obsession.

Music Quality Control

Our services resonate with one of the primary goals of every artist—to avoid listeners having access to their songs for free unless otherwise given permission. On that note, the we can assure every artist and record label that our services are safe and consistent. This is also one of the greatest ways to ensure protection for creators' contents: listening to your favorite song anytime, but only on demand without physical access of the song.
